Experience the Art of Drumming Online

Immerse yourself in the world of rhythm with our interactive virtual drum kit. Whether you're using your computer keyboard or the intuitive drum kit interface, you'll be able to create captivating beats effortlessly. To uncover the convenience of shortcuts, simply click "Show shortcuts" located above the drum kit, revealing a range of quick commands for both drums and cymbals.


Master the following shortcuts to enhance your drumming experience:

  • Tom 1: Press 'w'
  • Tom 2: Press 'a'
  • Tom 3: Press 's'
  • Tom 4: Press 'd'
  • Snare: Press 'j'
  • Crash: Press 'k'
  • Bass : Press 'l'

Drummers often utilize their right hand to command the cymbals (crash, ride, and hi-hat) and their left hand to dominate the snare drum. For efficiency when employing shortcuts, the left hand manages both the snare drum and the bass drum.
Delve into the world of virtual drums, and as your proficiency blossoms, channel your newfound skills onto a physical drum set. Harness the drum machine to uncover inspiration for innovative beats and rhythmic patterns. Elevate your practice sessions by harmonizing with your beloved tunes, turning your drumming journey into an enjoyable and enriching experience.
